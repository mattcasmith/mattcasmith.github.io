Recommendations

The best in books, films, TV series, and video games    

This is an evolving list of my recommended books, films, TV series, and video games. I have watched and read many more, but I consider these works to be the best of the best, either in their quality or particular personal impact.

Not all the entries below are critical darlings. They may not be considered the best in their respective subgenres - or even in their own series. But they're all extremely well put-together works and were thought-provoking in some way.

I'd gladly return to all of these titles given the time, but I'm also always looking for new favourites. If you have your own recommendations that you think I'd enjoy, please do reach out via email or message me on X.
